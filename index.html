CandidateAssess - AI-Powered Candidate Assessment Platform

What is CandidateAssess?

CandidateAssess is a freemium recruitment and candidate assessment platform designed for small and mid-sized teams with 1 to 100 employees. It helps companies streamline their hiring process by collecting applications via shareable links or QR codes, assessing candidates with AI-generated tests, managing candidate pools, and generating offer letters.

Target Audience

HR professionals, recruiters, hiring managers, startups, small businesses, and enterprises looking to simplify and accelerate their hiring process.

Core Features

  • Create unlimited job postings with detailed role descriptions
  • Share job openings via links and QR codes
  • AI-generated role-specific assessments and interview questions
  • Automated CV analysis and candidate scoring
  • Real-time candidate ranking and comparison
  • Offer letter generation and management
  • Candidate dashboard for application tracking

How It Works

  1. Create - Set up your job description. AI automatically generates unique assessments tailored to your role.
  2. Share - Share your job opening link on LinkedIn, job boards, email, or anywhere.
  3. Assess - Send unique assessments to candidates. 15 MCQs plus 4 open-ended questions scored instantly by AI.
  4. Hire - See ranked candidates with detailed scores. Send offer letters to your future teammates.

Pricing Plans

  • Starter: $29/month - 100 candidates/month, priority email support, CV analysis insights
  • Pro: $79/month - 250 candidates/month, advanced analytics, custom assessments, priority support

Problem We Solve

Traditional hiring is time-consuming, subjective, and often relies on gut feelings. CandidateAssess eliminates guesswork by providing objective, AI-powered candidate evaluations, saving time and improving hiring quality.
